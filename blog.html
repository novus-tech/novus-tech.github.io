Our Blogs

Lets talk autonomy.

March 3, 2018


Starting with the basics, we wanted to ensure that our robot will not, even accidentally, bump into anything. Although our main CPU will be calculating and providing speed to our real time CPU, our real time stm32 also has integrated Time of Flight sensors. These sensors sense the surrounding direction of the robot and apply emergency braking to the motors such that our robot always stops within a safe distance.

Batteries are Fundemental.

February 22, 2018


Our hardware has been severely bottlenecked by a larger problem we did not expect. One of our sponsors fell through and we were unable to successfully obtain our batteries in the last two months. We decided there was no point in waiting any longer and made the investment oursevles.. because what will our robot do without a power source? We also have attached a power distribution panel that will allow us to easily manage high current motors on different channels, with individual fuses, such that nothing goes beyond its appropriate limit. There is also a 120 A limit switch that ensures the battery never exceeds an appropriate power surge.

Object Detection - Realsense Camera

February 13, 2018


A key component to our robot being able to navigate the warehouse automatically is through the use of its eyes - the Realsense Camera. This camera provides rgb camera data along with depth data. This allows the robot to look for objects in its field of vision, and dynamically modifty the path.

Closed Loop Motor Control

January 31, 2018


The first step toward our drive train is making sure that we steer the system. This means making sure we can accurately turn the motor to the exact position, acting like a servo. Completing this allows us to appropriately turn without having to change driving speeds.

PCB Design

January 25, 2018


Heres a week dedicated to schematic and layout design of our Power and Communications Board. This development PCB houses both MCUs, our Intel UP autonomous algorithms, and stm32, our real time IO processing unit. This is going to be used as the central point where all inputs including power from battery, and all sensor inputs and communictions. All output signals including output controls to actuators is also through here. The PCBs have beene sent for production and all components ordered on DigiKey.
